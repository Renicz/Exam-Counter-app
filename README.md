# Exam-Counter-app
This app will help you 👍

**Exam Counter Pro** is an all-in-one study companion designed to help students stay organized, motivated, and focused throughout their academic journey.

Never lose track of your exams again. Simply add your upcoming exams, and the app will automatically display the number of days remaining, upcoming schedules, and countdowns so you always know what to prepare for.

### Key Features

📅 **Exam Countdown**

* Track unlimited exams with beautiful countdown timers.
* View upcoming exams at a glance.
* Get reminders before important exam dates.

⏱️ **Study Timer**

* Stay productive with a customizable study timer.
* Use focused study sessions to improve concentration.
* Perfect for Pomodoro and long study sessions.

🎯 **Focus Counter**

* Track your daily focus sessions.
* Build better study habits over time.
* Measure your consistency.

📝 **Study Planner**

* Plan your daily and weekly study schedule.
* Organize subjects, chapters, and revision sessions.
* Stay on top of your goals.

📆 **Calendar**

* View all exams, study plans, and important events in one place.
* Never miss deadlines or revision dates.

📊 **Analytics Dashboard**

* Monitor your study progress.
* View study time statistics.
* Analyze productivity trends with easy-to-read charts.

🔥 **Study Streaks**

* Build and maintain daily study streaks.
* Stay motivated by keeping your streak alive.
* Challenge yourself to study consistently.

🎖️ **More Smart Features**

* Clean and modern interface.
* Fast and lightweight performance.
* Easy exam management.
* Productivity-focused tools.
* Designed specifically for students.

Whether you're preparing for school exams, college entrance tests, board exams, or competitive exams, **Exam Counter Pro** helps you stay organized, focused, and motivated every day.
